
Octreotide Clinical Trial Landscape Summary

Dataset overview
----------------
The dataset contains 100 octreotide-related clinical trial records extracted from ClinicalTrials.gov.

----------------------

Based on recruitment status, 13 trials are classified as active or ongoing, while 87 trials are classified as inactive or closed.

------------------------
The most common development phase is PHASE2. This suggests that the octreotide clinical trial landscape includes substantial mid- to late-stage clinical development activity.
